About

Every knows it's important to eat healthy but the hard part is finding both tasty and healthy food.

The Pantry simplifies this process by suggesting healthy dinner plans for your entire week and creates your once a week grocery list.

How it Works:

  1. You enter your data and preferences
  2. The pantry choosing the best combination of tasty healthy meals for your lifestyle
  3. The recipes and ingridients are exported to Evernote, for easy offline access
