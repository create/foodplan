Pantry simplifies your life by taking care of your food needs. We generate a meal plan for you that balances your budget with a variety of healthy foods which meet expert-recommended daily and weekly nutrition goals.

How it Works:

1. Enter basic information, like how many people you cook for.

2. Instantly get your meals assigned to you. (Customize them afterwards if you want).

3. Get your ingredients delivered to you, via local services. (or get a shopping list pushed to your phone)
